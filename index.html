Movie Recommendations

Top movies of all time IMO

  1. Shawshank Redemption

    "Andy Dufresne - who crawled through a river of s*** and came out clean on the other side." Shawshank Redemption tops the rankings in our hearts as well as the IMDB ones, at an astonishing 9.3/10 topping the charts.

  2. Inception

    “You Mustn't Be Afraid To Dream A Little Bigger, Darling.” Inception is one of those mind bending ones that are a treat to watch and equally difficult if you try to understand. A true masterpiece.

  3. Detachment

    “A child's intelligent heart can fathom the depth of many darkplaces, but can it fathom the delicate moment of its own detachment?” Detachment is one of my personal favourites and also truely underrated. It's more of an art project with a message that should be an artist's must watch.
